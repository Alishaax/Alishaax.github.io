About Us!

Rosedale River Secondary school was founded in 1999 by Mala Koulen, Rosedale Rivers is a co-educational school,
semestered school located in Rosedale-Moore Park area. Our mission at Rosedale Rivers is to make our stundent feel
confindent in who they are and to make a healthy learning environment for them.

learning Programs at Rosedale Rivers

At Rosedale Rivers, we offer a wide range of programs for our student to learn from. These programs may inculde Specialist
High Skills Major Programs, Cooperative Education, Dual Credits (High School & College), and Ontario Youth Apprenticeship
Program (OYAP). Click the link at the bottom to learn more.
