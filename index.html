Welcome to Rosedale River Secondary School!

Rosedale River Secondary school was founded in 1999 by Mala Koulen, Rosedale Rivers is a co-educational school,
semestered school located in Rosedale-Moore Park area. Our mission at Rosedale Rivers is to make our stundent feel
confindent in who they are and to make a healthy learning environment for them.
